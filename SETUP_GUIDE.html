📝 Required: Contact Form Setup

Step 1: Go to https://web3forms.com/
Step 2: Sign up with your email (it's free!)
Step 3: Copy your Access Key
Step 4: Open contact.html in a text editor
Step 5: Find this line (around line 95):
<input type="hidden" name="access_key" value="YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE">
Step 6: Replace YOUR_ACCESS_KEY_HERE with your actual key
⚠️ Important: Without this step, the contact form won't send emails!
